About Us

GreenSpot Gardens is a premier outdoor dining and entertainment destination located in the heart of Kamakis, Ruiru along the Eastern Bypass. We offer a unique blend of traditional Kenyan hospitality and modern garden ambience.

Whether you're looking for the best Nyama Choma in town, a relaxed spot for drinks with friends, or a beautiful venue for your next private event, GreenSpot Gardens provides the perfect backdrop.

We prioritize your safety and convenience with a massive car park capable of hosting large groups, all guarded by our professional security team to ensure your peace of mind while you enjoy your time with us.

Our Vision

To be the leading garden restaurant in Kenya, known for quality food and an exceptional social experience.
